Design 15: Final Project

Challenge Statement:

Choose a theme or everyday object to create 8 designed pages using your theme or object as conceptual basis. These may work together as a narrative or be 8 individual, self-contained pages.The designed pages will be combined into either a hinged book, an accordion book, or placed within a portfolio specifically made for them.

Solution Statement

For this assignment I created a travel book showcasing different cities in different countries around the world. This was successful as each country was illustrated through one of the design techniques taught in class - black and white cut out shapes, cut outs using colored shapes, painting, having an interactive overlay, and a pop-up.

Materials used:

bristol paper, cutting knife, scissors, bone folder, cardboard, color aide paper, black paper, glue, string, and a needle.
