Design 15: Positive and Negative Space

Challenge Statement:

Create forms and shapes that elicit the qualities of organic vs. inorganic by cutting positive and negative shapes from a 5.5 x 8 piece of black paper and placing the cut out shapes on an 8x11 piece of white paper.

Solution Statement

To demonstrate the idea of positive and negative shapes, I decided to use the example of skulls and a taxi as they were examples of organic and non-organic subjects that are seen every day. For this project I used an X-acto knife to cut the shapes and reflected them across the middle of the page. By reflecting the opposite side of the image, it completes the picture allowing audiences to see the images from different perspectives, yet creating unity.

Materials used:

cutting knife, bristol paper, black paper
