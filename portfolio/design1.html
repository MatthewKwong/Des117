Design 1 Process Book

Challenge Statement:

Implement the design process and document your work while you join forces with fellow students to build an analog (physical, not digital) design suitable for inclusion in Emily Pilloton’s book Design Revolution. You will present a fully workable final prototype that demonstrates how your design will function.

Solution Statement

For this assignment I was put into a group with 3 other classmates where we were presented with designing an object for the category of “Play”. We were then to individually make our own process books presenting the design process behind what we made. What made my book successful was that I used a consistent playful color scheme and graphics that kept the theme of the book, but still professionally exhibited the process behind our product.
